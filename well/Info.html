Healthy Eating Information

Due to the issue of poor healthy eating, there are many resources available to those who seek to change their eating habits. A proper diet is necessary for you to live a long, happy, and healthy life. Along with the vast resources available, the government has many running programs to help guide you to clean, healthy diets. Below, is a list of some diets you can use to help in your overall health.

9 Diets for Overall Health

1. The Mediterranean Diet
2. The DASH Diet
3. Plant-based and Flexitarian Diets
4. The MIND Diet
5. WeightWatchers
6. Intermittent Fasting
7. The Volumetrics Diet
8. The Mayo Clinic Diet
9. Low Carb Diets

For more information on these diets, you can visit: healthline.com
